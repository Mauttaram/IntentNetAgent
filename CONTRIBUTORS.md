## Built on

**ScalableAgents** by Jaya Kompella
[github.com/Mauttaram/ScalableAgents](https://github.com/Mauttaram/ScalableAgents)

The full agent orchestration layer — LangGraph graph, HITL gates, CoT validator,
DAG executor, DispatchAdapter, and persistence — is provided by the ScalableAgents
framework. IntentNetAgent contributes the networking domain: schemas, tools, and demo.
